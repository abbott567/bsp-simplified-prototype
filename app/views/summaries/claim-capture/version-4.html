Research notes

Agents were still unsure where to find the information relating to where the claimant lived at the time of death, this is a theme that has continued to be seen throughout testing.

For the disallowed claim scenario agents didn't think that they needed to record all of the other eligibility criteria as being verified. They found the personal details helpful as they could use this for security questions but found everything else to be irrelevant. They also thought that as this claimant was over state pension age it would be flagged to them before they progressed with the claim so that they would know not to request additional information from the claimant.

Agents thought that the paused claim screen was indicating that they had not finished the claim they had just been working on. When they viewed the claim they could work out that this was a different claim and that it had an outstanding task to complete but it was still viewed as someone else's work appearing on 'their BSP Manager'
