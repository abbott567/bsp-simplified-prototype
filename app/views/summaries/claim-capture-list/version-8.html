Changes

  • Amended claim history to give more details about children situation
  • Amended confirm and claim details to include capture and verification seperately
  • Added title to partner details

Research notes

On the whole, the changes tested well.

Because we renamed 'Dependent children' to 'Children details', agents assumed they didn't need to fill this out because they could see from the claim form the claimant did not have children. This caused confusion.

There is content that asks the claimant to contact the claimant if they say they have dependent children, but have no Child Benefit number. Agents explained that they already have a process in place for this in the form of the letter, so the content may be redundant.

Claimants complained that flicking backwards and forwards through the paper form is annoying and confusing.
