Task List pattern

First iteration

In this prototype, we were looking at how agents might handle capturing a new claim. Unlike the linear journey, this version explores allowing the agents to process a claim in whichever order they like.

Research notes

Agents missed the reason for a claim being disallowed when viewing the claim details. This concerned them as a less experienced team member may not be aware how to tell from the claim details shown why this was the case.

As the tasks are now split into sections the date of death appears as a separate verification question. Agents were unsure why this information wasn't included with the partner details, especially as they can add payment details before confirming the death.

NI agents record their decisions on DMACR at the moment and when working through the prototype questions were asked around whether this would still be necessary.
