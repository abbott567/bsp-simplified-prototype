Research notes

Splitting initial information into a 3 page journey tested fine.

Bringing the partner details in caused no issues.

The marriage verification scenarios tested ok. But there was a bit of confusion as to whether this system or CAM would be handling BF's (paused claims). There was also some confusion around the ability of only being able to choose one place to verify the marriage as it could be in two systems.

There was some concern that there was no confirmation of disallowing a claim. Which means there was no way to go back if you made a mistake. It was a bit sudden.

As we had only focussed on the marriage verification, the death verification question was not consistent and this raised some questions.
