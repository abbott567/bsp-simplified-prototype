Changes

  • Renamed 'Children details back to 'Dependent children'
  • Removed content about contacting the claimant about Child Benefit
  • Scanned forms instead of paper ones

Research notes

Renaming the section seemed to test well. In previous sessions some agents were missing out the children details section because they thought if they didnt have children there would be nothing to fill in.

Testing with PDF's instead of paper forms was a great success. Agents were far more comfortable scrolling up and down than turning pages. This eliminated a lot of the frustrations about the form and the digital service not being in the same order.
