First iteration

In this prototype, we were looking at how agents might handle the processing of claims. The prototype looks at pulling a claim from a queue and trying to verify the evidence to put the claim into payment.

Research notes

In general, this tested well. It became apparent that there were several holes in the journey. For example, if you know for definitely they were never married, then you get stuck in a loop of permanantely pausing the claim.

There is more work to do around the system making decisions based on evidence, and also around the starting page as it is not clear whether process a claim or capture a claim is more important.

The payment scheduler might also need more work to allow a claim to be upgraded if Child Benefit is verified at a later date.
