Research notes

The way that agents can prevent payments mirrors what is being used now in the live service. Agents were therefore confident as to where to prevent payments and record the appropriate action (death/imprisonment). Agents noticed that the reason for preventing payments was listed in the claim history so were confident that they could deal with enquiries with the information provided. International group agents deal with returned payments often, therefore the reason that they select to prevent a schedule under these circumstances is not accurately represented. Potential for returned payments will be addressed in a separate story.
