This design worked much better for agents, there were no doubts about the outcome of the RVU decision and how to record this.

The partial backdating worked well, however not all agents checked the payment schedule before uprating the payments. The ones that did check the payment schedule had already worked out what the claimant should be entitled to based on the backdating rules. Agents were surprised but pleased that the effective date was being used to calculate the backdated payments so that manual intervention was not required. There were some comments around knowing which payments had been uprated for the purposes of creating the decision letter. This should be included in the next round of testing so that agents can work through the end to end process.
