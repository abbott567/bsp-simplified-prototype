Research notes

Agents noticed that the returned payments option was new in order to suspend (previously prevent) payments. Agents were comfortable navigating through this scenario despite it being completely new. They often enquired as to whether we had any new payment details from the claimant before continuing as this is their current business as usual approach. Agents were confident suspending the payments for the imprisonment scenario as this follows the same steps as preventing the payment.
