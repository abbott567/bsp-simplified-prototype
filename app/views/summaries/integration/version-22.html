Management Information (MI)

In this iteration, off the back of the last round of research, we have removed the oldest outstanding claims and have replaced it with 'claims cleared'. This was deemed more important by the people we tested with.

In this iteration we have also added two date inputs so agents can enter a date range to see information, as we found they need a breakdown to be more granular than just monthly.
