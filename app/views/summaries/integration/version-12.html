Hard stop

In this iteration we are looking at a hard stop for any claim where there is already an existing claim (that is allowed or unfinished) against that deceased nino.

Research notes

Agents were much more confident about drawing a conclusion as to what was going on with the two claims. They reached the conclusion that there were two spouses making a claim quite quickly in general without prompting. When asked what action they would take if they investigated and found the one in payment shouldn't be, they would refer the case to the over payment team and or fraud. The second claim wouldn't be recorded on the system and agents would need to pay it manually should it be the rightful claim.
