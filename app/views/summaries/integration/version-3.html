Research notes

Agents managed to change the payment details without issues. However, it was around a 60/40 split as to where they changed them from. 3/7 changed them from the claim details page and 4/7 changed them from the payment schedule.

There was some confusion as to why there was 3 change links when they all went to the same place, so we should look at reducing these to a single link once again.

Agents were a bit confused as to why they changed the details from the payment schedule page but ended up back on the claim details page.

Agents were frustrated that they couldn't see that the details had been updated in the claim history without goin back onto the claim details tab. May have to look at a way to show this on both the claim details and the payment schedule.
