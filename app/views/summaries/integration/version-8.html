Research notes

A banner was used to confirm that the change of rate had been actioned, a confirmation page to confirm details was also added. There were now also more potential questions and routes that agents could follow depending on the claimant circumstances (no/yes dependent children, no/yes to pregnancy) already captured as part of the claim. In this round of testing agents changed the rate via the claim details and were confident navigating through the questions presented and were happy that they had completed the journey. The banner used worked well but would impact upon other stories that involve displaying a claim history.
