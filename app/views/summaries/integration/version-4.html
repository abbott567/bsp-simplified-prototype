Research notes

4/5 agents changed the payment details from the claim details tab and 1/5 changed them from the payment schedule tab. Agents were more inclined to use the claim details section than during the previous round of testing. Agents recognised that the change history was a new addition to the payment schedule and they appreciated that this could show the most recent actions. Especially as once a claim is in payment actions are usually related to a change of circumstances and would therefore affect the payments. Agents understood how to change the payment details on an unfinished claim and were confident finding the appropriate way to do this.
