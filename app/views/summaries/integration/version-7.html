Late claim

First attempt

In this iteration we are looking at warning agents a claim is late, and amending the payment schedule accordingly.

Research notes

Some agents had already noticed that this was a late claim before entering the claim details. There wasn’t any resistance from agents about entering this onto the system whereas in the early stages of claim management agents weren’t keen to enter any details about a claim they knew would be disallowed. In terms of the information captured and displayed agents were confident that they would have all necessary information to deal with enquiries and wouldn’t be required to add any other verification that may have been provided (marriage certs etc).
